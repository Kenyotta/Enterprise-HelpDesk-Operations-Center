\# Department Directory

\# Apex Manufacturing, Inc.



\## Purpose



The Department Directory provides a centralized reference for all business units within Apex Manufacturing, Inc.



This document assists the Help Desk, Systems Administration, Cybersecurity, Human Resources, and IT Management teams by identifying department leadership, office locations, business hours, and primary IT contacts.



This directory should be referenced when routing support requests, assigning incidents, managing access requests, onboarding new employees, and coordinating enterprise projects.
