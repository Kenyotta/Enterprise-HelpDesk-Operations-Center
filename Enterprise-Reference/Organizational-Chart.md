\# Apex Manufacturing, Inc.



\## Organizational Overview



The organizational structure of Apex Manufacturing, Inc. is designed to support approximately 850 employees across multiple business locations.



Each department has designated leadership responsible for operational management while partnering with the Information Technology department to maintain secure and reliable business operations.



This organizational chart serves as the official reference for employee reporting relationships used throughout this repository.

\# IT Escalation Structure

```text
↓



Help Desk Supervisor



↓



Systems Administrator



↓



IT Infrastructure Manager



↓



Chief Information Officer

```



\---



\# After-Hours Support



Critical incidents occurring outside normal business hours are escalated according to the following order:



1\. Help Desk Supervisor

2\. Systems Administrator

3\. Network Administrator

4\. Cybersecurity Analyst (Security Incidents Only)

5\. IT Infrastructure Manager

6\. Chief Information Officer
