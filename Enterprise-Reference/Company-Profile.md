\# Apex Manufacturing, Inc.



\## Company Overview



Apex Manufacturing, Inc. is a fictional mid-sized manufacturing company created to simulate a realistic enterprise IT environment for documentation, training, and portfolio development.



The organization serves as the foundation for all systems, users, infrastructure, support procedures, security investigations, and enterprise projects contained throughout this repository.



The company operates a hybrid technology environment consisting of on-premises infrastructure and Microsoft cloud services, supporting employees across multiple departments and geographic locations.
