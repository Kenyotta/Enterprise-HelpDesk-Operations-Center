\# Asset Inventory



\---



\# Apex Manufacturing, Inc.



\## Asset Inventory



\### Purpose



This document serves as the official hardware asset inventory for Apex Manufacturing, Inc.



The inventory tracks company-owned technology assets assigned to employees across all business locations. It provides a centralized reference for Help Desk technicians, Systems Administrators, Cybersecurity personnel, and IT management.



This inventory supports:



\- Asset management

\- Help Desk operations

\- Device replacement

\- Hardware lifecycle management

\- Incident investigations

\- Security audits

\- Equipment deployment

\- Employee onboarding

\- Employee offboarding



\---



\# Asset Naming Standard



Company devices follow standardized naming conventions.



\## Laptops



```text

LT-<Department>-###



Examples



LT-IT-001

LT-FIN-014

LT-SLS-027

```



\---



\## Desktop Workstations



```text

DT-<Department>-###



Examples



DT-HR-003

DT-OPS-021

```



\---



\## Servers



```text

SRV-<Function>-##



Examples



SRV-DC-01

SRV-FILE-01

SRV-PRINT-01

```



\---



\## Printers



```text

PRN-<Location>-##



Examples



PRN-HQ-01

PRN-HR-01

PRN-FIN-01

```
