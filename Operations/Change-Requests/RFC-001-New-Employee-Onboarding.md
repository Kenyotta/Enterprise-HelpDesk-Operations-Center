\# Change Summary



Provision all required IT resources for a newly hired employee joining the Finance Department.



The employee requires standard user access, Microsoft 365 licensing, workstation deployment, network access, VPN connectivity, and department-specific permissions before their first day of employment.



\---



\# Business Justification



Providing all required technology resources before the employee's start date ensures business continuity, minimizes onboarding delays, and allows the employee to become productive immediately.
