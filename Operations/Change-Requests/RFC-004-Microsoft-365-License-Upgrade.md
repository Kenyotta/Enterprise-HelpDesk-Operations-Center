\# Change Summary



Upgrade a Finance Department employee's Microsoft 365 license from \*\*Business Standard\*\* to \*\*Microsoft 365 Business Premium\*\* to support enhanced security features, device management, and compliance requirements.



The change includes license reassignment, Microsoft Entra ID verification, Intune enrollment validation, and post-upgrade functionality testing.



\---



\# Business Justification



The employee has transitioned into a position requiring additional security controls, including:



\- Microsoft Intune device management

\- Conditional Access policies

\- Microsoft Defender for Business

\- Enhanced identity protection

\- Advanced security reporting



Upgrading the license ensures compliance with organizational security standards while enabling access to required enterprise services.

\# Risk Assessment



\## Overall Risk



\*\*Low\*\*



\### Risks



\- Temporary interruption of Microsoft 365 services

\- Incorrect license assignment

\- Delayed synchronization

\- Temporary application sign-out



\### Mitigation



\- Verify available license inventory

\- Perform change after business hours

\- Validate licensing before removing existing assignment

\- Monitor Microsoft 365 synchronization status
