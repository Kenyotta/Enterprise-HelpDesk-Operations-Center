\# Apex Manufacturing, Inc.



\## Information Technology Department



\### Shared Mailbox Request



\---



\# Purpose



This form is used to request the creation, modification, or removal of a Microsoft 365 Shared Mailbox.



Shared mailboxes allow multiple authorized employees to send, receive, and manage email from a common business email address without requiring a separate user license (subject to Microsoft licensing requirements).
