\# Apex Manufacturing, Inc.



\## Information Technology Department



\### Distribution Group Request



\---



\# Purpose



This form is used to request the creation, modification, or removal of Microsoft 365 Distribution Groups used for organizational email communications.



Distribution Groups allow employees to send email to multiple recipients using a single email address while supporting department, project, and company-wide communication.
