\# Apex Manufacturing, Inc.



\## Information Technology Department



\### Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) Reset Request



\---



\# Purpose



This form is used to request the reset or re-registration of Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for employees who are unable to authenticate using their registered authentication methods.



To protect company resources, the employee's identity must be verified before any MFA reset is performed.
