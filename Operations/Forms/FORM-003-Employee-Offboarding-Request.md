\# FORM-003 – Employee Offboarding Request



\---



\# Apex Manufacturing, Inc.



\## Information Technology Department



\### Employee Offboarding Request



\---



\# Purpose



This form is used to notify the Information Technology department of an employee's separation from Apex Manufacturing, Inc.



The form ensures all technology assets are recovered, user accounts are secured, access is revoked, and company data remains protected.



This request should be submitted immediately upon notification of an employee's resignation, termination, retirement, or contract completion.
