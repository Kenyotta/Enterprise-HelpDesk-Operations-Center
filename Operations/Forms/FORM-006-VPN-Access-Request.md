\# Apex Manufacturing, Inc.



\## Information Technology Department



\### VPN Access Request



\---



\# Purpose



This form is used to request Virtual Private Network (VPN) access for employees, contractors, and authorized third-party vendors requiring secure remote connectivity to Apex Manufacturing, Inc. resources.



VPN access is granted based on business necessity and must be approved by both management and Information Technology.
