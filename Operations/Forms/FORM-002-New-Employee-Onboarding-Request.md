\# FORM-002 – New Employee Onboarding Request



\---



\# Apex Manufacturing, Inc.



\## Information Technology Department



\### New Employee Onboarding Request



\---



\# Purpose



This form is used to request the creation of technology resources for newly hired employees.



The completed form provides the Information Technology department with the information required to prepare user accounts, assign hardware, configure system access, and ensure the employee is ready on their first day of employment.



This request should be submitted at least \*\*five (5) business days\*\* before the employee's start date.
